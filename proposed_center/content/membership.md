## Membership

## Become a member. Reap strategic rewards.

At the Center for Technology Management and Digital Leadership (CTMDL) at Northeastern University, our Corporate Membership program is a strategic platform that aligns business innovation with leadership development. It is a unique, dynamic space where education meets enterprise and thought leadership meets action. Membership provides companies with direct access to top emerging talent, tailored learning pathways, and—critically—new business opportunities through curated networking and collaboration.

In addition to the practical advantages, Corporate Membership also delivers meaningful brand-building benefits. Affiliation enhances employer branding, fosters talent attraction, and signals a strong commitment to future-ready leadership. Through this partnership, companies don’t just participate—they help shape the future of technology management and digital leadership. Membership signals a forward-thinking, learning-driven culture through engagement in thought leadership initiatives, custom learning experiences, and access to cutting-edge content. By contributing to and co-creating educational experiences, your organization becomes a recognized innovator and a valued contributor to the future of work.

In short, corporate membership is not only a high-value learning investment but also a powerful brand-building opportunity.

## Example: Nutanix

Nutanix, a $20B cloud computing software company based in San Jose, CA, [partnered with CTMDL](../shaping-executive-learning-nutanix-and-northeastern-universitys-digital-frontier/index.html) to sharpen its competitive advantage. Executives across the company embraced a customized program in Digital Disruption, Cybersecurity, Transformational Leadership, Data Science Strategy, The Future of Work, Digital Sustainability, and Artificial Intelligence, and positioned the company for long-term leadership in a brutally competitive space.

## Corporate Membership Benefits

- Access to a network of future-ready professionals and industry leaders
- Tailored learning and development pathways for your employees
- Opportunities to mentor and engage with graduate students
- Participation in exclusive member-only events and forums
- Brand exposure through showcases, panels, and faculty-led research collaborations

## B2B Networking and Strategic Partnership

Corporate Membership is a launchpad for strategic B2B collaboration. Through facilitated networking forums, innovation showcases, and peer exchange opportunities, members can:

- Forge new business partnerships
- Explore joint ventures and pilot programs
- Share challenges and co-develop solutions
- Engage in confidential, high-impact dialogues with other industry leaders

## Masterclasses that Drive Business Value

CTMDL masterclasses are not only for leadership development but also serve as high-value business development events. These curated sessions:

- Bring together your top clients and partners
- Facilitate crucial face time for strategic conversations
- Provide an intellectually stimulating environment to build trust
- Spark real business outcomes—new deals, partnerships, and service offerings

Each masterclass is co-designed with corporate members to reflect your business priorities and includes facilitation by leading experts. These are not just learning events—they are powerful business-building opportunities.

## Four Membership Tiers to Serve Organizations of All Sizes and Ambitions

Because every organization is different, CTMDL has created membership subscriptions that address specific needs, including increasingly deeper tuition discounts for employees, greater access to networking events, and more customized workshops and classes. The result is deeper, more profound and ultimately, more impactful change delivered to an increasingly larger cohort of your team.

## Bronze

- Access to all online courses

## Silver

- 20% discount for employees enrolling in MS program
- Access to members-only networking events (8 employees, clients, or partners)
- 2 customized workshops and masterclasses

## Gold

- 30% discount for employees enrolling in MS program
- Access to exclusive networking events (12 employees, clients, or partners)
- 3 customized workshops and masterclasses

## Platinum

- 50% discount for employees and partners enrolling in MS program
- Access to networking events (20 employees, clients, or partners)
- 4 customized workshops and masterclasses
