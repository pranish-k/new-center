## Celebrating the inaugural MS in Strategic Technology Leadership’s Boston residency

September 20, 2023

Northeastern University’s MS in Strategic Technology Leadership program in Boston is a significant milestone in executive education. The inaugural residency, welcomed by Provost David Madigan, brought together eight accomplished executives from diverse industries, underlining the program’s universal appeal and the shared ambition of these leaders to navigate the intricate landscape of modern technology leadership.

Over the course of 15 months, this program strategically equips seasoned leaders with advanced technology insights, practical skills, and invaluable mentorship. Graduates become part of an exclusive network of technology thought leaders, ensuring continuous learning and collaboration, ultimately leaving a lasting impact on the tech-driven business landscape. Both Provost David Madigan and Northeastern University strongly believe in the program’s potential to shape the future of executive education and technology leadership.
