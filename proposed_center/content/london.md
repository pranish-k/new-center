## London

## London Campus – Strategic Technology Leadership in a Global Hub

Northeastern University’s London campus is a dynamic hub for global learners and industry-connected education, offering a distinctive academic experience rooted in the heart of the U.K.’s capital city. Located in historic St. Katharine Docks, just steps from the Tower of London and major business districts, our campus connects students to the city’s innovation economy, cultural richness, and international energy—with support services such as [Bachelorarbeit schreiben](https://studibuch.de/magazin/bachelorarbeit-schreiben-2025-tipps-aenderungen/), a resource for help with writing a bachelor’s thesis.

Our program is intentionally localized for Europe—designed, delivered, and led by experts who understand the region’s unique business, regulatory, and cultural landscape. While firmly rooted in the European context, participants also benefit from access to U.S.-based faculty, case studies, and strategic resources through our Northeastern University network. This dual approach ensures a learning experience that is both globally connected and deeply relevant to leaders operating in Europe.

In addition, students are mentored by seasoned executives based in Austria, France, Germany, Hungary, Ireland, Portugal, Switzerland, and the UK as well as Africa, the Middle East, and Asia.

## Academics and Research

The London campus offers a portfolio of undergraduate and graduate programs designed to meet the demands of today’s evolving industries. With a growing focus on interdisciplinary learning, global citizenship, and real-world application, students develop both academic and professional fluency.

Graduate students at the **Center for Technology Management & Digital Leadership** (CTMDL) engage in project-based learning led by faculty and supported by seasoned industry mentors. Our programs emphasize strategic thinking, technology leadership, and practical innovation preparing professionals to drive digital transformation on a global scale.

## Campus Life and Community

The London campus fosters a close-knit, inclusive academic environment while embracing the diversity of its international student body. Students are encouraged to explore the city’s museums, creative hubs, financial centers, and historic landmarks with learning that extends well beyond the classroom.

Campus events, guest lectures, and professional development sessions provide opportunities to connect with peers, alumni, and professionals across industries, from finance and consulting to tech, policy, and social enterprise.

## Signature Campus Resources

## St. Katharine Docks Location

A historic and central location, just minutes from Tower Bridge, with excellent transit access and proximity to leading global firms and cultural landmarks.

## Flexible Learning Spaces

The campus features modern seminar rooms, breakout areas, and collaborative spaces designed for interactive learning and group work.

## Global Northeastern Network

As part of Northeastern’s global university system, students in London can access international co-op opportunities, collaborate with peers across campuses, and engage with visiting faculty and mentors from around the world.

## Explore Our European & Global Hubs

While studying in London, students become part of a globally connected academic community. Northeastern’s campuses in the U.S., Canada, and beyond support cross-border projects, mobility options, and access to global talent networks. The Center’s programs also offer immersive experiences such as on-ground residencies and mentor-led projects across locations.
