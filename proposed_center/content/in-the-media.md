## AI in times of 6G: Prof. Art Langer Interviewed by the CIO Match Podcast

In this episode of the CIO Match Video Podcast, Professor Art Langer explores the future of AI and 6G. As 6G technology continues to develop, Art discusses its expected rollout, challenges in global infrastructure, and the transformative potential it holds for industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and transportation. From real-time AI integration to the shifting landscape of digital infrastructure, this conversation provides key insights for IT leaders preparing for the next wave of technological disruption. Tune in to discover how companies can stay ahead in the race toward 6G innovation!
