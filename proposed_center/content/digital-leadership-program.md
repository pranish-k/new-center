## Program Overview

The Digital Leadership Experience at Northeastern University’s Center for Technology Management and Digital Leadership empowers executives, board members, entrepreneurs, intrapreneurs, and post-career professionals to enhance their effectiveness in launching or managing complex digital transformation initiatives. Our customized approach combines world-class faculty instruction with one-on-one mentoring from industry leaders.

- → Personalized education plan tailored to your busy schedule
