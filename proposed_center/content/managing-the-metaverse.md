## Managing AR, VR, & Metaverse

## Course Introduction

Step into the evolving world of the metaverse with a comprehensive look at its origins, current applications, and future potential. This course begins by breaking down key concepts and tracing the evolution of the metaverse from early virtual environments to its expanding role across industries such as marketing, product design, retail, social media, fashion, and gaming.

Participants will explore current metaverse technologies, projected advancements, and the design and infrastructure challenges that shape its future. Through guided exercises and discussions, you’ll conceptualize metaverse experiences and develop ideas tailored to a specific industry of your choice. Whether you’re new to the topic or looking to deepen your understanding, this course offers practical insight into navigating and leveraging the metaverse.

## Course Description

This course explores the foundations of the metaverse and its growing influence on technology, society, and business. It examines the historical evolution and convergence of augmented reality (AR), virtual reality (VR), and alternate digital realities, as well as their potential to shape immersive, connected experiences.

Using transmedia storytelling as a guiding framework, participants will investigate how these technologies influence human interaction and open up new creative and commercial opportunities in the digital space.
