## Smart City Initiatives - Business Opportunities

## Course Introduction

Discover how emerging technologies are shaping the future of urban living—and learn to lead smart city initiatives that tackle real challenges in transportation, energy, and infrastructure while creating sustainable business value for your organization and community.

## Why Choose the “Smart City Initiatives - Business Opportunities” Course at Northeastern?

***Real-World Urban Challenges:***

Engage with practical examples of how cities are solving issues related to transportation, energy, and infrastructure using emerging technologies.

***Sustainable Innovation Focus:***

Explore how smart technologies can improve quality of life while advancing sustainability and long-term development goals.

***Business-Centric Perspective:***

Understand how organizational leadership and business strategy play a critical role in the success of smart city projects.

***Interactive and Actionable Learning:***

Participate in exercises and discussions that help you define smart city solutions relevant to your organization and community.
