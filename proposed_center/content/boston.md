## Boston

## Welcome to Northeastern’s Boston Campus

Northeastern’s Boston campus is the university’s founding and largest hub—home to a thriving community of learners, researchers, and industry collaborators. Located in the heart of the city, the Boston campus connects students to a diverse network of tech, healthcare, science, and business sectors while offering an immersive urban academic experience.

With more than 44,000 students and 110,000+ alumni in Greater Boston, the campus serves as a launchpad for global impact through interdisciplinary learning, collaborative research, and community engagement.

## Academics and Research

The Boston campus offers Northeastern’s broadest selection of academic programs—including undergraduate, graduate, and PhD degrees across disciplines. Students engage in cutting-edge research alongside world-class institutions, hospitals, and innovation-driven companies in science, engineering, healthcare, business, and more.

Signature facilities like the Interdisciplinary Science and Engineering Complex foster high-impact collaborations, while research and co-op partnerships support student innovation and real-world problem-solving across global industries.

## Campus Life and Community

Join 44,000 students—including 21,000+ undergraduates and 18,000+ graduate students—on a vibrant urban campus. Get involved in 400+ student clubs and organizations and connect with 110,000+ alumni in Greater Boston. Experience the packed Huskies DogHouse on game nights and explore public art throughout campus.
