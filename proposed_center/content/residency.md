## Residency

## Executive Residencies: Immersive. Strategic. Transformational.

Our executive residencies are designed to take you beyond the classroom and into environments where ideas are tested, leadership is practiced, and collaboration is key. Through thoughtfully structured in-person experiences, you’ll explore market and technology dynamics, product realization, strategic planning, and operations — alongside faculty and peers who bring rich, real-world insight to every session.

Each residency spans four days of full-day sessions and hands-on learning. They are hosted at Northeastern’s dynamic global campuses in Boston and London — cities that serve as living case studies for leadership, innovation, and industry transformation.

Please note: Travel and hotel accommodations are not included in tuition and must be arranged independently.
